Are you a creator on Instagram or TikTok? Do you love productivity hacks, cutting-edge AI, and beautifully designed native desktop experiences? Let's build the future of desktop workflow automation together.

What We Offer

  • Premium Compensation: We pay anywhere from $30 to $2,000 per video. The exact rate is flexible and scales based on your audience size, video production value, and historical engagement metrics.

Requirements

  • Active Audiences: Creators on Instagram Reels and TikTok.
  • High Engagement: We prioritize creators who have exceptional engagement rates and a track record of videos with millions of views. We seek genuine viewer connection over empty follower counts.
  • Focus Areas: Tech tutorials, developer setups, design workflows, lifehacks, macOS customization, or productivity tips.

Contact Us

If you're ready to partner with us, please reach out to our partnerships lead at creators@glide.inc with a link to your profiles, your media kit (including engagement rate screenshots), and a brief sentence on why you use Glide.
