# Brand Memory — Wispr Flow & Crank

This document captures the brand assets, design philosophy, and typography hierarchy extracted from `https://wisprflow.ai/` to align Crank's visual presence with modern, premium editorial design standards.

---

## 🎨 Visual Identity & Style Guide

### 1. Typography (The Editorial Contrast)
Wispr Flow achieves its high-end, premium look by pairing a traditional, elegant Serif display font with a clean, modern, readable Sans-Serif font:
*   **Headings / Display Text:** **EB Garamond** (Google Fonts). Elegant, high-contrast, classic serif. Gives a literary, high-trust, human-centric vibe. Used with italic highlights to emphasize key actions.
*   **Body / UI Text:** **Figtree** (Google Fonts) or **Plus Jakarta Sans**. Warm, geometric sans-serif. Highly readable at small sizes.
*   **Code / Technical Labels:** **JetBrains Mono**. Clean monospace.

### 2. Color Palette (Organic Minimalist)
Rather than typical cyber-neon sci-fi glowing colors, the brand focuses on warm, organic, editorial colors that convey privacy and maturity:
*   **Primary Background:** Warm Off-Black (`#0b0b0a` or `#0e0e0d`).
*   **Primary Text:** Crisp Warm White (`#fbfbfa`).
*   **Accent Teal:** Emerald Forest Green (`#044f46` / `#00b49d`).
*   **Muted Text:** Warm Sage Grey (`#878782` or `#8f928e`).
*   **Light Mode Fallback:** Warm Cream (`#fcfcf9` background, `#121210` text).

### 3. Layout & Negative Space
*   **Breathing Room:** Extreme use of negative space. Generous section padding (`120px` to `160px`) to prevent clutter.
*   **Borders:** Hairline borders (`1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)`) creating a grid grid-guide bounding frame.
*   **Curves as Ornaments:** Minimalist, organic curved path diagrams (e.g. tracking waves of unpolished speech converting into a straight line of polished text).

## ⚡ Key Product Messaging (Wispr Flow)
*   **Core Promise:** "Don't type, just speak" / "Effortless Voice Dictation".
*   **Key Benefit:** "Turns speech into clear, polished writing in every app. Removes filler words, rambles, and typos automatically. 4x faster than typing."
*   **Device Support:** "Available on Mac, Windows, iPhone, and Android."

## 🛠️ Application to Crank Landing Page
To adapt Crank to this premium brand standard:
1.  **Typography Swap:** Load `EB Garamond` and `Figtree` from Google Fonts. Change headers to EB Garamond and buttons/body text to Figtree.
2.  **Color Alignment:** Swap neon gradients for warm off-black backdrops and a deep forest-green/teal active highlight color.
3.  **Visual Asset:** Incorporate a wave curve representing voice translation directly inside the mock desktop screen or hero background.
4.  **Refined Buttons:** Update buttons to have sharper corners (`6px` border-radius) and warm neutral borders, dropping round pills for a cleaner structural feel.
