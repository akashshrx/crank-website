Privacy Policy

At Glide, we prioritize your privacy and the protection of your data. We are committed to safeguarding all data collected, both personal and non-personal, adhering to the highest global standards of data protection. Our policies are designed to keep your personal data under your control while maintaining compliance with relevant frameworks, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

This Privacy Policy applies to the Glide website and application service, governing the use of services provided by Glide to its users.

1. Notice

Whenever we collect information that could be used to personally identify you, we will explicitly ask for your provision and permission. This policy ensures that you are fully informed and consenting to the collection of your personal data. Such requests occur when explicit confirmation of your permission is required to proceed, such as signing in with your Google account or managing your billing profile.

2. Your Consent

You always have the option to grant or withhold permission for us to process your information. If you opt not to provide essential information required for our service (such as Google authentication or billing details), we will not be able to grant you access to the features of Glide that depend on this information.

3. Usage & Processing

3.1 Transient Payloads (Processed in the Moment)

Glide operates with a modern cloud-based architecture to deliver high-agency capabilities. When you invoke commands or trigger workflows, payloads containing screenshots, active application contexts, and voice or text prompts are transiently transmitted through our secure backend to our AI providers. We do NOT store or persist your prompts, chat history, or screen contexts anywhere on our servers. Payloads are processed on-the-fly to return your response and are immediately discarded.

3.2 Authentication & Billing Data

We collect and retain limited data necessary for accounts and subscription management:

  • Google OAuth: We authenticate users via Google Sign-In. We store basic metadata associated with your Google account (such as email address and name) to verify access.
  • Stripe: We utilize Stripe for payment and subscription processing. Billing details and transaction identifiers are handled securely by Stripe in accordance with their industry standards.

3.3 Third-Party AI Providers

Glide relies on third-party AI partners (including Anthropic, OpenAI, and ElevenLabs) to power assistant features, voice outputs, and system actions. Payload contexts are securely proxied to these partners for immediate response generation. We do not sell your data under any circumstances.

3.4 Data Security

All transient payloads and retained credential metadata are encrypted during transit (using Transport Layer Security/TLS) and protected with industry-standard security software and practices.

4. Retention of Information

We retain account and billing identifiers for as long as necessary to provide you with Glide's services. All personally identifiable information will be deleted upon account deletion. Following your request, your account data will be permanently purged within 14 days, except where extended retention is required to fulfill legal compliance obligations.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with data protection regulations (including GDPR), you are entitled to several rights regarding your data:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personally identifiable information we retain.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct or adjust any inaccurate metadata.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of all personally identifiable information we store.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request that we stop processing your information if it is inaccurate or unlawfully handled.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email link below.

6. Contact

Should you have any inquiries or comments regarding our Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@glide.inc.
